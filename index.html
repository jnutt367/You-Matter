Created By:

Jasmine Meade and Jason Nutt

Mission:

The application will provide resources to the users that steers them in the right direction to receive the help they need. Or, in emergency situations gives them a pipeline to an actual person and/or community to provide support in dealing with these things that no one was ever meant to go through alone anyways.

Features:

There will be a chat bot that interacts with the user that we have programmed as many relevant questions that a person in a situations such as there's may have...

What brings you to our site?

Are you suffering from...?

Do you need to talk with someone now?...

Resources:

We are in the process of looking the best resources that we can, with wide ranges of help, to direct these men and women. It is our goal to provide every resource we can that we believe may help to improve and/or advance the person that seeks it a better way of life or at least a lifeline of support to lean on until they can achieve this goal.

A list of possible resources:

Websites with similar mission statements,

Reading Material: articles, books and blogs.

Interview demographic that we are trying to help.

Use our own unique life experience, empathy and understanding to incorporate the things that are most essential for a path to peace and recovery of your spirit.
